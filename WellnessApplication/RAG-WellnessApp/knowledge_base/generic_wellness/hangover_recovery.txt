HANGOVER RECOVERY

What is a hangover:
A hangover is your body's reaction to too much alcohol. Symptoms include headache, nausea, fatigue, dehydration.

What to do:
1. Drink water - lots of it. Alcohol dehydrates you.
2. Eat bland foods - toast, crackers, bananas. Easy on stomach.
3. Rest - sleep helps recovery.
4. Pain relief - ibuprofen for headache (not acetaminophen, hard on liver).
5. Electrolytes - sports drinks or coconut water.

What NOT to do:
- More alcohol (hair of the dog makes it worse)
- Heavy exercise (you're dehydrated)
- Skip eating (low blood sugar makes it worse)

When to see doctor:
- Severe vomiting (can't keep water down)
- Confusion or severe headache
- Irregular heartbeat
- Vomiting blood

Prevention:
- Drink water between alcoholic drinks
- Eat before drinking
- Pace yourself
- Know your limits

Recovery time:
Usually 24 hours. If longer, see doctor.
