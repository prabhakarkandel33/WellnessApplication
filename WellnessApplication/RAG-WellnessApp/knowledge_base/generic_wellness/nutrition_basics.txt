NUTRITION BASICS

What is nutrition:
Nutrition is the process of consuming and using food to support growth, energy, and overall health. A balanced diet provides essential nutrients like carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Key guidelines:
1. Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables daily.  
2. Include whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.  
3. Limit added sugars, salt, and processed foods.  
4. Stay hydrated with water.  
5. Practice portion control and mindful eating.  

What NOT to do:
- Skip meals regularly.  
- Overconsume junk food or sugary drinks.  
- Rely on supplements as a replacement for balanced food.  

When to see a nutritionist:
- Persistent digestive issues  
- Unexplained weight loss or gain  
- Specific dietary needs due to medical conditions  

Benefits of good nutrition:
- Improved energy and concentration  
- Healthy weight management  
- Strong immune system  
- Better overall physical and mental health
