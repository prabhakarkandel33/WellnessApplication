HYDRATION IMPORTANCE

Why hydration matters:
Water is essential for all body functions including digestion, circulation, temperature regulation, and joint lubrication. Proper hydration improves energy, focus, and skin health.

How to stay hydrated:
1. Drink water regularly throughout the day.
2. Eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables.
3. Replace lost fluids after exercise or heat exposure.
4. Limit excessive caffeine and alcohol, which can dehydrate.

Signs of dehydration:
- Thirst, dry mouth, dark urine
- Fatigue or dizziness
- Headaches or poor concentration
- Dry skin

Benefits of staying hydrated:
- Better energy and alertness
- Healthy skin
- Proper digestion and kidney function
- Reduced risk of headaches and cramps

Recommended intake:
Usually 2–3 liters per day, depending on activity, climate, and individual needs. Adjust based on thirst and body signals.
