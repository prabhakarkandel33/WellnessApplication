CAFFEINE EFFECTS

What is caffeine:
Caffeine is a natural stimulant found in coffee, tea, chocolate, and some energy drinks. It affects the central nervous system, increasing alertness and reducing fatigue.

Effects of caffeine:
1. Increases alertness and concentration.  
2. Can temporarily boost mood and energy.  
3. May improve physical performance in moderate amounts.  
4. Diuretic effect – can increase urine output.  
5. Can cause jitteriness, anxiety, or insomnia if overconsumed.  

What NOT to do:
- Consume in excess, especially late in the day.  
- Rely on caffeine as a substitute for sleep.  
- Mix with alcohol.  

When to see a doctor:
- Heart palpitations, dizziness, or severe anxiety after caffeine intake.  
- Persistent insomnia or sleep disturbances.  

Recommended intake:
Moderate amounts (up to 400 mg per day for most adults) are generally safe; individual tolerance varies.
