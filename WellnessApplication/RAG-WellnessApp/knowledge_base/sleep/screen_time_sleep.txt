SCREEN TIME SLEEP

What is screen time sleep:
Exposure to screens before bed can disrupt melatonin production and make falling asleep harder.

What to do:
1. Limit screen use at least 1 hour before bed.  
2. Use blue light filters or night mode in the evening.  
3. Read a book or listen to calming music instead.  
4. Keep electronic devices out of the bedroom if possible.

What NOT to do:
- Use bright screens right before sleep.  
- Keep phones or tablets in the bedroom while sleeping.

Benefits:
- Easier to fall asleep.  
- Improved sleep quality.  
- Less eye strain and mental stimulation before bed.
