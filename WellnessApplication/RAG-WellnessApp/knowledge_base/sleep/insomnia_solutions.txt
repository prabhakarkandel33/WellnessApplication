INSOMNIA SOLUTIONS

What is insomnia solution:
Practical steps to help improve sleep and reduce insomnia symptoms.

What to do:
1. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.  
2. Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing or meditation.  
3. Limit caffeine, nicotine, and alcohol in the evening.  
4. Create a comfortable sleep environment that is dark, quiet, and cool.  
5. Avoid large meals before bedtime.  
6. Exercise regularly but not right before bed.

What NOT to do:
- Stay in bed awake for long periods.  
- Rely on sleep medications without consulting a doctor.

When to see a doctor:
- Insomnia lasting more than a few weeks.  
- Severe daytime impairment.  
- Possible underlying medical or mental health conditions.
