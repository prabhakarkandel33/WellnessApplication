SLEEP MEDITATION

What is sleep meditation:
Meditation techniques help calm the mind, reduce stress, and prepare the body for sleep.

What to do:
1. Find a quiet, comfortable space.  
2. Focus on deep breathing or guided meditation.  
3. Use apps or audio recordings if needed.  
4. Practice 10–20 minutes before bedtime.  
5. Combine with other good sleep hygiene habits.

Benefits:
- Easier to fall asleep.  
- Improved sleep quality.  
- Reduced anxiety and stress.  
- Better overall relaxation.
