SLEEP SCHEDULE FIX

What is sleep schedule fix:
Steps to adjust your sleep and wake times to improve sleep quality.

What to do:
1. Set a consistent bedtime and wake-up time, even on weekends.  
2. Gradually adjust your sleep time by 15–30 minutes if needed.  
3. Get morning sunlight exposure to regulate your internal clock.  
4. Avoid naps late in the day.  
5. Limit screen time and bright lights before bed.

What NOT to do:
- Make sudden drastic changes to your sleep schedule.  
- Use caffeine late in the day.  
- Ignore persistent sleep difficulties.

Benefits:
- Better sleep quality.  
- Improved energy and focus.  
- Reduced daytime fatigue.
