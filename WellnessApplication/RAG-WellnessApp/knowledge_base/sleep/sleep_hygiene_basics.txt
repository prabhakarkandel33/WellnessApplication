SLEEP HYGIENE BASICS

What is sleep hygiene:
Sleep hygiene is a set of habits and practices that improve sleep quality and duration.

What to do:
1. Keep a consistent sleep and wake time.  
2. Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bed.  
3. Limit screen time at least 1 hour before sleep.  
4. Make your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool.  
5. Use the bed only for sleep and intimacy.

What NOT to do:
- Nap excessively late in the day.  
- Smoke or drink alcohol before sleep.

Benefits:
- Better sleep quality.  
- Increased energy and focus.  
- Improved mood and overall health.
