SLEEP ENVIRONMENT

What is sleep environment:
A proper sleep environment supports falling asleep quickly and staying asleep.

What to do:
1. Keep the bedroom dark and quiet.  
2. Maintain a cool temperature (around 18–22°C).  
3. Use comfortable mattress and pillows.  
4. Minimize noise and distractions.  
5. Reserve the bed only for sleep and intimacy.

What NOT to do:
- Sleep in a cluttered, noisy, or bright room.  
- Use the bed for work or other non-sleep activities.

Benefits:
- Easier to fall asleep.  
- Fewer awakenings.  
- Better overall sleep quality.
