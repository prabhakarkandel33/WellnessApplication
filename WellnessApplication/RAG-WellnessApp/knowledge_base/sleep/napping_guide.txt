NAPPING GUIDE

What is a nap:
A short period of sleep during the day to restore energy and alertness.

What to do:
1. Keep naps 10–30 minutes to avoid grogginess.  
2. Nap early in the day, before 3 PM.  
3. Find a quiet, comfortable place.  
4. Use an alarm to prevent oversleeping.

What NOT to do:
- Nap too late in the day.  
- Nap longer than 30–45 minutes routinely.

Benefits:
- Increased energy and focus.  
- Reduced stress.  
- Better cognitive performance.
