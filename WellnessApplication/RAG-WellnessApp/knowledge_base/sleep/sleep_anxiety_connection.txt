SLEEP ANXIETY CONNECTION

What is sleep anxiety:
Worry or stress about not sleeping, which can worsen insomnia.

What to do:
1. Practice relaxation techniques before bed.  
2. Keep a consistent sleep schedule.  
3. Avoid clock-watching in bed.  
4. Journal thoughts earlier in the evening to reduce worry.  
5. Limit caffeine and alcohol.

What NOT to do:
- Stay awake worrying in bed.  
- Consume stimulants before sleep.

Effects:
- Difficulty falling asleep.  
- Poor sleep quality.  
- Increased daytime fatigue and irritability.
