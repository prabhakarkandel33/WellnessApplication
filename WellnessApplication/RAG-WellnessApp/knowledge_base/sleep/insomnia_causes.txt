INSOMNIA CAUSES

What is insomnia:
Insomnia is difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or waking too early, leading to daytime fatigue or impairment.

What causes insomnia:
- Stress, anxiety, or depression.  
- Poor sleep environment or habits.  
- Caffeine, nicotine, or alcohol.  
- Medical conditions such as pain, sleep apnea, or restless legs.  
- Irregular schedules or shift work.

Effects:
- Fatigue and irritability.  
- Poor concentration.  
- Reduced immune function.  
- Increased risk of chronic health issues.
