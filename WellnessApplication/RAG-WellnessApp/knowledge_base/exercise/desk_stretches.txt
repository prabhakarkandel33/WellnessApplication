DESK STRETCHES

What are desk stretches:
Simple stretches done while working at a desk.

What to do:
1. Neck stretches.
2. Shoulder rolls.
3. Wrist and hand stretches.
4. Stand and stretch periodically.

What NOT to do:
- Stay seated for long periods.
- Ignore posture.

Benefits:
- Reduces stiffness.
- Improves posture.
- Prevents desk-related pain.
