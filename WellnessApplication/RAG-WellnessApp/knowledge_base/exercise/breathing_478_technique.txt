4-7-8 BREATHING

What is 4-7-8 breathing:
A breathing technique to promote relaxation and sleep.

What to do:
1. Inhale through nose for 4 seconds.
2. Hold breath for 7 seconds.
3. Exhale slowly through mouth for 8 seconds.
4. Repeat 4 times.

What NOT to do:
- Rush the breathing cycle.
- Practice while driving.

Benefits:
- Reduces anxiety.
- Helps with sleep.
