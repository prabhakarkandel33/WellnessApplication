WALKING BENEFITS

What is walking:
Walking is a low-impact physical activity suitable for most people.

What to do:
1. Walk at a comfortable pace.
2. Aim for at least 20–30 minutes daily.
3. Maintain good posture while walking.

What NOT to do:
- Ignore pain or discomfort.
- Walk in unsafe environments.

Benefits:
- Improves cardiovascular health.
- Boosts mood and energy.
- Supports weight management.
- Improves joint mobility.
