BOX BREATHING

What is box breathing:
A breathing technique to calm the nervous system using equal breathing intervals.

What to do:
1. Inhale for 4 seconds.
2. Hold breath for 4 seconds.
3. Exhale for 4 seconds.
4. Hold breath for 4 seconds.
5. Repeat for several minutes.

What NOT to do:
- Force your breath.
- Practice while dizzy.

Benefits:
- Reduces stress.
- Improves focus.
- Calms anxiety.
