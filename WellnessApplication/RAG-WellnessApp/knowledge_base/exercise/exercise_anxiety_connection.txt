EXERCISE ANXIETY CONNECTION

What is exercise anxiety connection:
The relationship between physical activity and anxiety levels.

What to do:
1. Engage in regular moderate exercise.
2. Focus on breathing during workouts.
3. Choose low-pressure activities.

What NOT to do:
- Overtrain.
- Exercise to exhaustion.

Benefits:
- Reduces anxiety symptoms.
- Improves mood.
- Enhances stress resilience.
