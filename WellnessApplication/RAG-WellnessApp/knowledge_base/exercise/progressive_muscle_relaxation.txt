PROGRESSIVE MUSCLE RELAXATION

What is progressive muscle relaxation:
A technique that reduces tension by tightening and relaxing muscles.

What to do:
1. Tense one muscle group for 5 seconds.
2. Relax for 10 seconds.
3. Move through all major muscle groups.

What NOT to do:
- Hold breath.
- Tense to the point of pain.

Benefits:
- Reduces stress.
- Improves sleep.
- Relieves muscle tension.
