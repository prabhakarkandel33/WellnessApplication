EVENING STRETCHING

What is evening stretching:
Stretching to relax muscles and prepare for rest.

What to do:
1. Stretch legs and lower back.
2. Hold stretches for 20–30 seconds.
3. Breathe deeply.

What NOT to do:
- Bounce during stretches.
- Stretch into pain.

Benefits:
- Reduces muscle tension.
- Improves sleep quality.
