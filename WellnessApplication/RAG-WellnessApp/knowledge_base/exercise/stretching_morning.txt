MORNING STRETCHING

What is morning stretching:
Gentle stretches to wake up muscles and joints.

What to do:
1. Stretch neck and shoulders.
2. Stretch back and hips.
3. Move slowly and gently.

What NOT to do:
- Stretch aggressively.
- Skip breathing.

Benefits:
- Improves flexibility.
- Reduces morning stiffness.
- Boosts energy.
