YOGA FOR BEGINNERS

What is beginner yoga:
Gentle yoga practices focused on flexibility, balance, and breathing.

What to do:
1. Start with simple poses.
2. Focus on breathing.
3. Practice 10–20 minutes daily.
4. Use a mat for comfort.

What NOT to do:
- Force difficult poses.
- Ignore pain.

Benefits:
- Improves flexibility.
- Reduces stress.
- Builds body awareness.
