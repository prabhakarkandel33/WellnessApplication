WALKING HOW TO START

What is starting walking:
Beginning a walking routine gradually to build consistency.

What to do:
1. Start with 5–10 minutes per day.
2. Increase duration slowly each week.
3. Wear comfortable shoes.
4. Choose flat, safe routes.

What NOT to do:
- Start too fast or too long.
- Ignore warm-up and cool-down.

Benefits:
- Reduces injury risk.
- Builds long-term habit.
