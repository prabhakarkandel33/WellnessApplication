YOGA FOR ANXIETY

What is yoga for anxiety:
Yoga poses that promote relaxation and calm the nervous system.

What to do:
1. Practice child’s pose.
2. Practice seated forward fold.
3. Practice legs-up-the-wall.
4. Breathe slowly during poses.

What NOT to do:
- Rush between poses.
- Hold breath.

Benefits:
- Reduces anxiety.
- Improves emotional regulation.
