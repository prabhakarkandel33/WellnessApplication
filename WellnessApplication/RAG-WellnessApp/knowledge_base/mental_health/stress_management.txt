STRESS MANAGEMENT

What is stress management:
Strategies to reduce and cope with stress.

What to do:
1. Practice deep breathing or meditation.
2. Exercise regularly.
3. Prioritize sleep and nutrition.
4. Set boundaries and manage time.

What NOT to do:
- Overcommit constantly.
- Avoid addressing stressors.

Benefits:
- Reduced anxiety and burnout.
- Improved well-being.
