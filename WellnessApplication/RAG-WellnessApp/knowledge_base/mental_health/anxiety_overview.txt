ANXIETY OVERVIEW

What is anxiety:
Anxiety is a normal stress response that becomes problematic when excessive or persistent.

What to do:
1. Recognize anxiety triggers.
2. Practice relaxation and breathing techniques.
3. Maintain healthy sleep and exercise habits.

What NOT to do:
- Avoid all situations due to fear.
- Ignore persistent anxiety.

Benefits:
- Better emotional control.
- Improved daily functioning.
