ANXIETY BREATHING

What is anxiety breathing:
Breathing techniques used to calm the nervous system during anxiety.

What to do:
1. Inhale slowly through the nose.
2. Exhale slowly through the mouth.
3. Use box breathing or 4-7-8 breathing.
4. Practice for several minutes.

What NOT to do:
- Hold breath forcefully.
- Hyperventilate.

Benefits:
- Reduces anxiety intensity.
- Lowers heart rate.
