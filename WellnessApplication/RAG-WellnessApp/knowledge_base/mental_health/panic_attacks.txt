PANIC ATTACKS

What are panic attacks:
Sudden intense episodes of fear with physical symptoms.

Common symptoms:
- Rapid heartbeat.
- Chest pain.
- Shortness of breath.
- Dizziness.

What to do:
1. Focus on slow breathing.
2. Ground yourself in surroundings.
3. Remind yourself the attack will pass.

When to see a doctor:
- Frequent or unexplained panic attacks.
