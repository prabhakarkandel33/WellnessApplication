MISSED ACTIVITIES

What to do when you miss activities:
Missing a session or activity is normal; the key is to get back on track.

What to do:
1. Resume the next scheduled activity.  
2. Adjust intensity if needed.  
3. Log missed activities for review.  
4. Reflect on causes to prevent future misses.

What NOT to do:
- Skip multiple days without resuming.  
- Feel guilty or give up entirely.

Benefits:
- Maintains program consistency.  
- Encourages flexibility.  
- Supports long-term adherence.
