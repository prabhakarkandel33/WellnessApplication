SWITCHING PROGRAMS

What is switching programs:
Moving from one wellness plan to another while maintaining continuity.

What to do:
1. Evaluate reasons for switching.  
2. Review new program goals and structure.  
3. Gradually transition to new activities.  
4. Keep tracking progress during the switch.

What NOT to do:
- Jump abruptly to a completely different program.  
- Ignore previous progress.

Benefits:
- Smooth transition.  
- Maintains healthy habits.  
- Reduces risk of burnout or injury.
