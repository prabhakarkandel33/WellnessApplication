HOW PROGRAMS WORK

What is a wellness program:
A wellness program is a structured plan designed to improve physical, mental, and emotional health.

How programs work:
1. Provide daily or weekly guidance for activities.  
2. Include exercise, nutrition, sleep, and mental wellness components.  
3. Track user progress and adapt recommendations.  
4. Offer reminders, tips, and motivation.

What NOT to do:
- Ignore program instructions completely.  
- Skip core activities without reason.

Benefits:
- Supports healthy habits.  
- Builds consistency.  
- Improves overall well-being.
