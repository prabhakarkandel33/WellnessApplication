WHEN TO SEEK PROFESSIONAL HELP

What is professional help:
Guidance from healthcare providers, therapists, or certified wellness professionals.

When to seek help:
1. Persistent pain, fatigue, or unusual symptoms.  
2. Mental health concerns like anxiety, depression, or severe stress.  
3. Difficulty following the program due to medical conditions.  
4. Questions about nutrition, supplements, or exercise safety.

What NOT to do:
- Ignore warning signs.  
- Self-diagnose serious conditions.

Benefits:
- Ensures safety.  
- Provides personalized guidance.  
- Prevents worsening of health conditions.
