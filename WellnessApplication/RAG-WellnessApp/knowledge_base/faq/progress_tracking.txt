PROGRESS TRACKING

What is progress tracking:
Monitoring user activity and improvements over time in a wellness program.

What to do:
1. Log completed exercises, meals, and sleep.  
2. Record notes about mood, energy, or symptoms.  
3. Review weekly progress reports.  
4. Adjust goals based on tracked data.

What NOT to do:
- Skip logging consistently.  
- Compare progress to others.

Benefits:
- Identifies trends and improvements.  
- Keeps users accountable.  
- Helps personalize program recommendations.
